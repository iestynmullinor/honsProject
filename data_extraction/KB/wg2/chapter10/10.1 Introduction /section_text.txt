Adaptation in energy sector is becoming increasingly crucial in the Asian region, which has been assessed in a new subsection.Adaptation technology and innovations are also of high importance for the region. Classification of adaptation technology and its use in different systems are assessed.On the governance side, the nexus approach among several systems like food, energy and water is highlighted, and its importance is assessed.New concepts on decentralised and self-reliant society, such as the Circulating and Ecological Sphere (CES), are emerging for integrated adaptive governance.As a part of a sustainable development pathway, interlinkages of climate-change adaptation (CCA) and disaster risk reduction (DRR) are highlighted.
